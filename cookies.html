Cookie Recipe

Cookie recipe that makes some amount of cookies

Incrediants

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • n Vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Hot Water
  • 1 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Set oven to 350F or 175C
  2. Use electic mixer to mix the butter and both types of sugar
  3. Mix the eggs and vanilla
  4. Mix baking soda and hot water
  5. Mix chocolate chips
  6. Bake for at least 10 minutes
